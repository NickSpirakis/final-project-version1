Getting Started

To Play Hangman you need to guess the correct letters of the word.

Have a friend choose a word and enter it in the text field below "Type a Word".

Have your friend click "Set a Word" to confirm it, and have them pass you the computer.

Your goal is to use the text field below "Guess a Letter" to type in guessed letters.

You may only guess one letter at a time.

Incorrect guesses will appear in the "Wrong Guesses" field.

You gain 3 Points towards your score for getting a guess right, but you loose 1 Point for every letter you guess wrong!

If you feel stuck, use the "Hint" button!

Press "Clear Current Word" to let someone choose a new word, while adding on to your previous scores.

Press "Reset All" to start over.

Click Close Modal button to close instructions, or click anywhere outside of the modal.
